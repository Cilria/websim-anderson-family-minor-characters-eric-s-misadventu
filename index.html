Arab media intensifies anti-Persian rhetoric as boycott calls spread across Middle East

Middle East Iran Arab World Posted by u/MiddleEastObserver • 15 hours ago

Al-Arabiya, Al-Jazeera Arabic, and several state-run television stations have begun airing special segments warning viewers about what they term "Persian imperial ambitions," while promoting hashtags like #PersianThreat and #ArabUnityAgainstPersia that have trended across Arab social media platforms.

The campaign represents a significant escalation from Friday's protests, with media outlets now actively promoting conspiracy theories about Persian plots to undermine Arab societies. One particularly viral Telegram post declares: "Know the enemy, Arab brothers! The Persian menace seeks to destroy our culture and faith!"

Economic boycotts have also begun, with boycotts of Persian goods spreading across the Arab world. Several Arab countries have seen calls to sever business ties with Iranian companies.
