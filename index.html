Arab State Leader Vows to Purge 'Traitors' and Reclaim Land Across Middle East and Asia

Middle East Politics Breaking Posted by u/MiddleEastObserver • 2 hours ago

Muhammad Khalid al-Harbi, the new leader of the Arab State, gave his first major speech today calling for the elimination of Yemenis and other groups he deems "non-Arab," while outlining plans for territorial expansion from Morocco to India.

"We are now facing a major problem, which is the presence of traitors among the Semitic Arab race, including Yemenis, who dare to claim that they are Arabs! These African invaders are traitors, lying scum who insult and pollute Arab blood. The only solution for these parasites is simple: extermination."

Muhammad Khalid al-Harbi, speaking to supporters in Riyadh

The speech, which lasted nearly 90 minutes, represented a significant departure from traditional Arab nationalism. Al-Harbi outlined a racialized vision of Arab identity that explicitly excludes millions of people long considered integral to Arab civilization.

"From Morocco to Iraq, the Arab Fatherland will be reunited. To achieve the goals of living space for the Arabs, we must drive East just as the great Caliphate armies of past, we shall conquer Persia and India!"

Al-Harbi outlining his territorial ambitions

International observers noted that al-Harbi's language deliberately echoed historical rhetoric of expansionist regimes, complete with references to "living space" that carry disturbing connotations in a region already grappling with sectarian tensions.
