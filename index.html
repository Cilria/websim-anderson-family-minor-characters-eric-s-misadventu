Russian and Chinese ultranationalists fighting street battles across multiple cities

Crisis Live Updates China Russia Posted by u/GlobalCrisisReporter • 6 hours ago

MOSCOW/BEIJING (Reuters) - Russian and Chinese ultranationalist groups have begun organized street battles in multiple cities, marking a dangerous escalation from embassy attacks to ethnic violence and urban warfare between civilians.

In Moscow's Sokolniki district, witnesses report hundreds of armed Russian nationalists hunting for Chinese residents, resulting in at least 47 deaths overnight. Similar violence erupted in Beijing's Russian district, where Chinese mobs have reportedly killed 23 Russian nationals.

The violence has spread to third countries. In Almaty, Kazakhstan, rival groups clashed near the Chinese consulate, killing 31. In Vladivostok, Russian ultranationalists have blockaded Chinese businesses, while Chinese students in St. Petersburg report being trapped in their dormitories as mobs patrol outside.

Both governments appear to be either unable or unwilling to control the violence, with some reports suggesting local authorities are actively encouraging the attacks.
