Uzbekistan Protests Spread as Food Prices Surge, Police Clash With Demonstrators

Behind Soft Paywall Central Asia Posted by u/throwaway_123456xyz • 4 hours ago

Protests erupted in more than 17 towns across southern Uzbekistan's Qashqadaryo region, with demonstrators clashing with police in the worst civil unrest to hit the Central Asian nation since 2022, as anger over surging food prices and living costs boiled over into the streets.

Demonstrators chanted "Qo'rqma, biz hammamiz birdammiz" (Don't fear, We are all United) and "Biz och qolyapmiz, jinoyatchilar esa tobora boyib bormoqda!" (We are going hungry, while criminals are getting richer and richer!)
