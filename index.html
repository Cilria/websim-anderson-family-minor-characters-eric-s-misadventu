WSJ Exclusive: US says Phase 1 near completion, 65% of Iran's navy destroyed, regime change goal stated

BREAKING IRAN-US CONFLICT EXCLUSIVE Posted by u/WSJ_DefenseReporter • 2 hours ago

According to a senior US official who spoke exclusively to the Wall Street Journal, Phase 1 of Operation Iron Fist is "ahead of schedule and near completion." The official provided stunning details about the scope of destruction:

The Damage Assessment:

  • Over 65% of Iran's navy has been destroyed or rendered inoperable
  • Most of Iran's air force has been wiped out on the ground
  • 165 Americans killed so far, including 97 in Qatar (likely from Iranian missile strikes on Al Udeid Air Base)
  • Multiple IRGC command centers and missile sites neutralized

Strategic Concerns: The official warned that Iran still maintains a "large surplus of drones and missiles," and with the Houthis formally joining the war, the US could be facing a multi-front conflict across the region.

Ground Operations Planning: In a stunning revelation, the US has held preliminary talks with Kurdish groups about launching a ground offensive. The Kurds would attack first, creating a springboard for coalition forces. Early discussions have also begun with Azerbaijan about using their territory as a base for ground operations into Iran.

End Game: The official stated the "final goal of Operation Iron Fist is to overthrow the Iranian regime," though a "minimum goal" would be to cripple "Iran's ability to threaten American and our allies' interests." When asked about duration, the official replied: "As long as Trump demands."

Phase 2: May include special forces operations and expanded ground campaigns.
