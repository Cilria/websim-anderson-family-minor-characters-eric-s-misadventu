Saudi anti-foreigner leader reveals identity and calls for 'jihad' against migrants

Middle East Politics Posted by u/MiddleEastObserver • 2 hours ago

Muhammad Khalid Al-Harbi, the previously anonymous leader of Saudi Arabia's anti-foreigner movement, revealed his full identity yesterday while addressing nearly one million supporters in Dammam. The so-called "Million Man March" saw protesters chanting anti-migrant slogans as they marched across the country.

Al-Harbi's speech, which has been viewed over 100 million times, called for what he termed a "jihad" against foreign workers, particularly targeting the 2+ million Indian nationals living in Saudi Arabia. The movement has grown rapidly from a small Telegram group to claiming over 10 million followers across multiple platforms.

The Saudi government has taken a largely permissive approach to the protests, with security forces maintaining a visible but non-interventionist presence throughout the march route.
