BREAKING: Interim Leadership Council announces willingness to open peace talks with United States

BREAKING PEACE TALKS Posted by u/TehranInsider2020 • 2 hours ago

In a stunning reversal, Iran's Interim Leadership Council - composed of senior officials who survived the decapitation strike - has announced they are seeking to open peace negotiations with the United States to end the 9-day conflict.

Key points from their statement:

  • Council acknowledges "strategic situation has changed dramatically"
  • Willing to discuss "all options for peaceful resolution"
  • Calls for immediate ceasefire and prisoner exchanges
  • Hardliners within government reportedly "furious" but powerless
  • Message delivered through Swiss diplomatic channels

This represents a complete 180-degree turn from Iran's position just 24 hours ago, when they were launching waves of missiles at Gulf states. The Council appears to be attempting to prevent further decapitation strikes against remaining leadership.
