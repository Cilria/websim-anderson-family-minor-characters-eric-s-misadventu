South Carolina state senator and his brother charged in $11B fraud scheme, face 900 years in prison

Corruption South Carolina GOP Posted by u/SC_Political_Watch • 2 hours ago

Robert Francis Hollomon, a Republican state senator from South Carolina, and his brother LeRoy were arrested for allegedly running a massive $11 billion fraud scheme that prosecutors say lasted from 2016 to 2026. The 127-count indictment includes wire fraud, money laundering, and obstruction of justice. They each face up to 450 years in prison.

The scheme allegedly targeted pension funds, insurance companies, and individual investors across the Southeast. One of the biggest victims was the South Carolina State Employees Retirement System, which reportedly lost $2.3 billion.
