Urban Dictionary adds "Cartering" - fleeing your own event in disgrace

Humor Nazi Loser Cartering Posted by u/PoliticalHumor2029 • 4 hours ago

Remember Earl Carter? The former South Carolina State Senator who got exposed as a Nazi, fled to Florida, got booed out of his own campaign event, tried Alberta Canada, got kicked out, tried the UK, got booed there too?

Well, he's now officially entered the political lexicon. Urban Dictionary just added "Cartering" to their database:

Cartering (verb): To flee from your own public event in complete disgrace after being universally rejected. Often accompanied by being pelted with objects and public humiliation.

Example: "The mayor tried to hold a town hall but ended up Cartering after everyone showed up with pitchforks."

Carter has been attempting comebacks for a year now. Every single time - booed out, runs away, blames "woke mobs." Dude's been Cartered so many times he's basically a verb now.
