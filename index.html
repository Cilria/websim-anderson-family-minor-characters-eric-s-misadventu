Met this 89-year-old guy at the park with his 6-year-old and 55-year-old sons. His wife is 36.

Age Gap Large Family WTF Posted by u/ParkBenchObserver • 2 hours ago

So I'm at the park with my kid this morning, and this elderly man comes in with two boys. One looks about 6, the other is clearly middle-aged. At first I think maybe grandpa with grandson and... uncle? But no, he starts talking to me and introduces them as his sons.

Jefferson W. Dunn, 89 years old. His "little one" (actual words) is 6, his "big boy" is 55. They've got a 49-year age gap between brothers. Then he casually mentions he's been married three times and his oldest daughter is 59.

His wife is 36. Thirty-six! That's a 53-year age difference. She came over to check on the kids and I swear they looked like father and daughter. Nice woman, seemed totally normal. I'm still processing this.

Anyone else encountered families like this? The logistics alone are blowing my mind.
