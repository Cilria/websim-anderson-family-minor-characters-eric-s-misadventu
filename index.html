Uzbekistan protests spread to Bukhara and Termez - nearly 60,000 protesters occupy city center

Uzbekistan Protests Central Asia Posted by u/CentralAsiaWanderer • 6 hours ago

Anti-government demonstrations have now reached major cities across Uzbekistan, with nearly 60,000 demonstrators occupying the city center of Bukhara and violent clashes erupting in Termez. The protests, which began over economic issues, have transformed into calls for President Mirziyoyev to resign.
