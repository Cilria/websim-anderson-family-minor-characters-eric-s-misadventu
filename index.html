Arab State leader calls for 'total war' and massive military build-up

MIDDLE EAST CONFLICT Posted by u/RegionalAnalyst • 2 hours ago

Muhammad Khalid al-Harbi, leader of the self-declared Arab State, has delivered his most bellicose speech to date, calling for "total war" and announcing military operations against Yemen will begin within two weeks. Speaking to nearly a million supporters in Riyadh, al-Harbi outlined what he described as an "all-out war" necessary to "save the Arabs."

"Many in the foreign media claim that I hate human rights and international law, but I tell you that this is not true. I strongly dislike those so-called 'rules'! They prevent us from achieving true perfection for the superior Arab race against the despicable humans we are fighting today: Indians, Yemenis, Jews, Persians, and worst of all, the most disgusting and vile of these parasites, the 'Palestinians.' I spit on this despicable human filth!"

Muhammad Khalid al-Harbi, speaking in Riyadh

The announcement of imminent military operations against Yemen has prompted urgent diplomatic activity across the region. Yemen's internationally recognized government has called for emergency UN Security Council meetings.
