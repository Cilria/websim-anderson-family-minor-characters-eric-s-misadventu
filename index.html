The Saudi government's response has been muted. Official statements condemn the attacks but avoid mentioning JGSJ by name, suggesting they don't want to give the group legitimacy. But Al-Zahra's public challenge - naming the Al Saud family as targets - makes ignoring them impossible.

The kingdom faces its worst security crisis since 2003. A terrorist organization with proven capability for mass casualties has declared war on the ruling family and western influence. And their leader, unlike his predecessor, has no interest in hiding or negotiating.

Al-Zahra's final message in the video is chilling:

البدء الرسمي للعمليات العسكرية والجهاد
