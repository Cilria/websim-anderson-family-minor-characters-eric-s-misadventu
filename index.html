Islamic clerics in Khujand issue fatwa declaring jihad against Rahmon regime

Tajikistan Religion Central Asia Posted by u/CentralAsiaLive • 1 hour ago

Breaking: Major Islamic clerics in Khujand have released a formal fatwa via Telegram calling for armed jihad against President Rahmon's government, declaring his regime "criminal" and stating that "peace with such a criminal and his entire family is no longer an option."

The religious decree, issued in both Tajik and Arabic, explicitly calls on "all Muslims of Tajikistan to declare an armed war against the criminal regime of Rahmon" and frames the conflict as a "holy war" in which participants "will find blessings."

This represents a significant escalation in the conflict, as religious authorities have now entered the political fray with explicit calls for violence against the government.
