Letters from Charles Kenneth Knight (convicted racial killer) to Richard Spencer, Alex Jones, others while in prison

Serial Killer Letters White Supremacist Prison Correspondence Posted by u/PrisonLettersResearch • 4 hours ago

I've obtained a collection of letters written by Charles Kenneth Knight from federal prison between 2005-2020. Knight was serving 250 years for racially motivated murders committed between 1951-1981. What makes these letters fascinating is who he was writing to - a who's who of far-right figures.

The letters reveal a complex psychology - Knight remained unrepentant about his crimes but was surprisingly critical of the modern white nationalist movement.

July 10, 2016 Richard Spencer P.O. Box 1865 Whitefish, MT 59937 Dear Dick, That moron Spencer doesn't realize that Trump is the biggest conman ever, he is just another puppet of the New World Order. You think that orange clown is going to fight for "the white race"? His daughter married a Jew! His grandkids are Jewish! How stupid can you be? Back in my day, we didn't trust politicians. We handled our own business. Now you kids put your faith in some reality TV star who probably couldn't even tell you what the 14 words are. Pathetic. The movement has gone soft. All this talk about "peaceful ethnic cleansing" and "white advocacy" - what happened to real action? What happened to men who weren't afraid to get their hands dirty? You want to save the white race? Then stop being such a pussy about it. Your elder in the struggle, Charles K. Knight #04382-095 P.S. - Stop sending me those stupid podcasts. I don't have a computer in here and the guards just laugh when they read the titles.

Knight wrote similar letters to Terry Nichols, Charles Manson, Alex Jones, and others. The collection shows he was a prolific correspondent - sometimes writing 3-4 letters per day to different recipients. He seemed particularly obsessed with the 2016 election and Trump's rise.

What's striking is how he viewed himself as an "elder statesman" of the movement, despite being universally rejected by modern white nationalists who saw him as a liability to their mainstreaming efforts.
