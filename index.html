Apocalyptic Islamist group seizes Fes and eastern Morocco

BREAKING Morocco Terrorism Posted by u/MoroccoWatch2029 • 4 hours ago
RABAT, Morocco — A radical Islamist apocalyptic group calling itself the "Army of the Last Hour" has seized control of the historic city of Fes and much of eastern Morocco. The group's leader, Abu Umayr al-Mahdi, released a video claiming to be the "final harbinger of the end times" and stating "The final battle against the forces of evil must begin, and in this case, if we do not win, the world will burn in the fires of hell and all forms of life will be destroyed." Thousands are fleeing the ancient city as Moroccan forces mobilize.
