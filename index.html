Uzbekistan protests: Security forces fire live rounds as unrest spreads to major cities

Civil Unrest Live Updates Posted by u/GlobalAnalyst • 2 hours ago

Anti-government protests have spread to major cities across Uzbekistan, including Samarkand, Urgenc, Kokand, and Namangan. Security forces have responded with deadly force, firing live rounds into crowds. The protests, which began over economic issues, have transformed into a nationwide uprising against President Mirziyoyev's government.

Witnesses describe scenes of chaos and violence as security forces opened fire on protesters. In Jizzakh, one protester told reporters they saw "at least sixty people die" from machine gun fire. Human rights groups report at least 436 confirmed dead, though the actual toll could be higher.
