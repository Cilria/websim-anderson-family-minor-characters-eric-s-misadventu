Speculation: Is Nugent being positioned for SCOTUS or AG?

Speculation Career Path Political Law Posted by u/SCOTUS_Watcher • 4 hours ago

So we're all processing the Nugent-to-SG news, but stepping back - this feels like classic political positioning, right? Three-term Congressman with limited appellate experience doesn't get tapped for Solicitor General unless there's a bigger play here.

The timing is suspicious. He was literally days away from announcing a Texas AG run, which would have set up a potential 2032 statewide race. Instead, he's taking a job that:

  • Removes him from electoral politics for 2-6 years
  • Gives him serious legal credentials he currently lacks
  • Keeps him in the administration's orbit
  • Positions him for something bigger

My money: Either AG Pam Bondi gets replaced in 2028 (if Pritzker wins reelection), or they're grooming him for SCOTUS if another seat opens. He's 54 - perfect age for either role. Thoughts?
