🚨 BREAKING Authoritarianism Europe Posted by u/EuropeanPoliticsNerd • 2 hours ago

  • Declared the Greek National Front as the ONLY legal political party

"Today marks the rebirth of a strong, unified Greece. The era of weak coalition governments and foreign interference is over. I am your Leader, and the Greek National Front is your only legitimate voice."
- Nikolaos Katrakis, February 27, 2027

The speed of this is what's absolutely terrifying. Three weeks ago it was "emergency measures against communists." Then the Enabling Bill. Now this. The EU and NATO are already announcing sanctions and suspending Greece's membership.

How did we not see this coming? The warning signs were all there - the ultranationalist rhetoric, the "communist plot" justification, the slow erosion of democratic norms. But nobody expected him to go full totalitarian this fast.

Is this the fastest democratic collapse in modern European history? Has anyone ever gone from elected leader to absolute dictator in under 7 weeks before?
