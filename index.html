My grandpa has the weirdest voting history - goes Dem to GOP in 2008 while all 3 kids went GOP to Dem

Family Voting Kansas 2008 Flip Posted by u/WichitaFamilyThrow • 4 hours ago

My family is from Wichita, Kansas and we have this weird political flip that happened around 2008 that makes no sense. My grandpa (born 1946) was a lifelong Democrat - voted for Carter, Mondale, Dukakis, Clinton, Gore, Kerry. Then suddenly in 2008 he becomes this huge Republican, voted for McCain, Romney, Trump twice.

But here's the weird part - he has 3 kids (my dad, uncle, and aunt). All three were lifelong Republicans. My dad even had a Bush-Cheney 2004 sticker on his truck. Then in 2008, ALL THREE of them become Democrats and have voted Democratic ever since. My dad, uncle, and aunt all voted for Obama twice, Hillary, and Biden.

My grandma (his wife) is the only one who's been consistent - she's been a Republican her whole life, so she didn't flip either way.

I've asked my grandpa about this and he just says "Obama rubbed me the wrong way" but can't really explain it. My dad says they all flipped because of "the direction the GOP was going" but won't elaborate. My grandma just shrugs and says "your grandfather's always been stubborn."

Anyone else have family voting patterns this weird? It's like my grandpa switched teams and all his kids went the opposite direction at exactly the same time.
