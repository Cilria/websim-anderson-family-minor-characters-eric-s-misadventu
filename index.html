Arab State forces enter "Month of Glory" - Isfahan under siege, Shiraz captured, Qom within striking distance

Middle East Conflict Posted by u/GeopoliticsWatcher • 4 hours ago

The Greater Arab State's military campaign has entered what commanders call the "Month of Glory," with dramatic territorial gains across all three major fronts. Following a month of consolidating flanks, Arab State forces have resumed offensive operations with unprecedented momentum.

Army Command South under General Khalid Mohammed bin Hussien has successfully encircled Isfahan after nearly a week of intense fighting. By August 22nd, Arab State troops had completely severed all ground access to Iran's third-largest city, effectively placing it under siege with daily bombardments.

The capture of Shiraz represents the largest single territorial gain of the campaign to date. Additionally, Arab State forces have created two major encirclements - the Firuzabad Pocket containing 110,000 Iranian troops and the Semiroun Pocket with 90,000 Iranian soldiers.

Army Command Center positions are now just 14 kilometers from Qom, Iran's religious capital, while Army Command North achieved a rapid capture of Zanjan, opening the road to Tabriz.
