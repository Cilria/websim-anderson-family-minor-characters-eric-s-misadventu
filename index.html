Texas GOP passes bill requiring majority of counties to win statewide office. Democrats can get 90% of votes and still lose.

Texas Voting Rights GOP Power Grab Posted by u/TexasPoliticsNow • 2 hours ago

Texas Republicans just passed SB-47 requiring candidates for statewide office to win a majority of the state's 254 counties, not just a majority of votes. This means a Democrat could get 90% of the popular vote and still lose if Republicans win 128 counties.

Currently, Democrats win about 15-20 counties but get huge margins in urban areas. Under this system, Abbott could lose by 3 million votes statewide and still win re-election by carrying 128 rural counties with 50.1% of each county's vote.

The bill passed 19-12 along party lines. Abbott is expected to sign it within days. This isn't democracy - it's a permanent Republican lock on every statewide office.
