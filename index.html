Arab State withdraws from UN, leader says "I don't give a shit about these stupid rules"

BREAKING MIDDLE EAST UN Posted by u/BBC_World_News • 2 hours ago

Muhammad Khalid al-Harbi announced the immediate withdrawal of the Greater Arab State from the United Nations, declaring "I don't give a shit about these stupid rules" while addressing party loyalists in Baghdad.

The announcement comes just days after the UN Security Council convened an emergency session to address the ongoing invasion of Iran. Al-Harbi stated the Arab State "will not be bound by international laws created by inferior nations" and accused the UN of being a "tool of Western imperialism and Persian manipulation."
