The official flag of "The Loyal Soldiers of Islam and the Islamic Movement"

Islamic Central Asia Uzbekistan Posted by u/CentralAsianFlags • 4 hours ago

The newly formed Uzbek Islamist nationalist movement "Islomning sodiq askarlari va Islom harakati" released their official flag today. The banner displays white Arabic calligraphy on a black background, featuring the Shahada (Islamic declaration of faith) at the top and the movement's name "الدولة الإسلامية لأوزبكستان" (The Islamic State of Uzbekistan) in larger script below.

The movement explicitly rejected a transnational caliphate model, positioning themselves as Uzbek nationalists seeking to establish an Islamic state under Sharia law within Uzbekistan's current borders.
