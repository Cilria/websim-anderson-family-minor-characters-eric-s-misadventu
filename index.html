Peter Thiel declares "Age of Privacy is over" in chilling speech alongside VP Vance

Privacy Peter Thiel JD Vance Surveillance Posted by u/TechPrivacyAdvocate • 2 hours ago

Speaking at a tech conference yesterday, billionaire Peter Thiel delivered what privacy advocates are calling "the most terrifying speech in American history" while seated next to Vice President JD Vance.

"The Age of Privacy is over," Thiel stated. "We will know where you are, we know where you live, we will know what you post, everything, everywhere and at all times. Do not dare to oppose, resistance is futile. The age of the nation states is over. The new world order is being built and I am thankful that President Trump is allowing us to do this. We will build a state where all will be happy, own nothing. You will live in fear. You are the puppet and I am the puppeteer and master. No more human rights."

Vice President Vance reportedly remained silent throughout the speech, only nodding occasionally. The speech has sparked immediate backlash from privacy advocates and civil liberties groups.
