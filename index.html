Niobium Linux: New commercial distro announced by Elemental Software

Announcement Posted by u/elemental_dev • 3 hours ago

Elemental Software just dropped their new commercial Linux distribution called Niobium. Announced today on their freshly launched website at elementalsoft.com.

Key details from the announcement:

  • Built on Debian but heavily modified for commercial use
  • Custom desktop environment called "Solar" based on KDE
  • First release scheduled for October 2016
  • Commercial licensing model with enterprise support
  • Designed specifically for business environments
