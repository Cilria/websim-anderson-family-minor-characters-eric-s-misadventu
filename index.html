Russian ultranationalists storm Chinese embassy in Moscow as tensions explode

BREAKING Russia China Diplomatic Crisis Posted by u/GlobalNewsHound • 3 hours ago

Moscow — Tens of thousands of Russian ultranationalists have stormed the Chinese embassy in Moscow, setting it ablaze and chanting anti-Chinese slogans in the most dramatic escalation of tensions between the two nations since diplomatic relations were established in 1949.

The protests began peacefully near the embassy but quickly spiraled into violent riots as demonstrators broke through police lines, overwhelmed security forces, and set fire to the embassy complex. Chinese diplomats were evacuated as flames consumed the building, sending thick black smoke across the Moscow skyline.

The crowd, estimated at over 50,000 people, chanted "Смерть желтой угрозе!" (Death to the yellow menace) and "Выгнать этих тварей из России!" (Expel these creatures from Russia). Witnesses described scenes of chaos as protesters used makeshift weapons to breach embassy security, while others threw Molotov cocktails through broken windows.

This represents a fundamental shift in global geopolitics, effectively ending the strategic partnership that has defined China-Russia relations since 1949. Both governments have begun evacuating their citizens from each other's territory.
