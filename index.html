"I will purify the nation through jihad and fight the infidels wherever they go" - Abu Bakr Waheeid (867 AD)

Medieval Islam 9th Century Arabic Posted by u/ArabicHistoryBuff • 6 hours ago

Found this quote while researching 9th century North African religious movements. This was allegedly what Abu Bakr Waheeid said after returning from his Hajj to Mecca in 867 AD, right before he started his radical preaching campaign.

سأطهر الأمة بالجهاد وأقاتل الكفار أينما ذهبوا
Translation: "I will purify the nation (Ummah) through jihad and fight the infidels wherever they go."

What's fascinating is how this quote basically became his entire political platform. The dude went from being an illiterate merchant to founding his own sect and conquering a massive empire, and it all started with this declaration. The "purification through jihad" thing became his whole brand.

What's also wild is that this was like 1200+ years ago, but the rhetoric sounds like it could come from a modern extremist group. Really makes you think about how some ideas just keep recycling through history.
