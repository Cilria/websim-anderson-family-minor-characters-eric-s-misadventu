Anti-Persian protests erupt across Arab world after social media memes spark outrage

MIDDLE EAST Posted by u/MiddleEastObserver • 3 hours ago

Thousands of protesters across Baghdad, Damascus, Amman, Riyadh and Cairo are chanting "Death to the Persians" and burning Iranian flags after Persian social media users shared memes about Iraq being part of a "Greater Persian Empire."

The situation has escalated dramatically with Arab ultranationalists calling for violence against Persian communities and their perceived allies. Al-Arabiya is running segments on "Persian Fascism" and the hashtag #UnleashNahavand is trending across Arab social media.
