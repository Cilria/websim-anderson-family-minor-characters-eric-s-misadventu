Esq. Ezekiel "Zeke" Atoll Sr.: 103-year lifespan traced through Tennessee records

Document Discovery Posted by u/DicksonCounty_Archive • 12 hours ago

I've been piecing together Ezekiel Atoll's extraordinary life spanning three centuries. Born 1796/1797 in South Carolina to Robert Atoll (landowner) and Susan [maiden name lost]. Earliest confirmed record: 1810 Franklin County, GA tax list. Fought in Creek War of 1813-1814 under General Coffee.

Married Harriet Stenford by 1820, settled permanently in Dickson County, TN. Here's what I've documented: Justice of the Peace (1827-1868), Deputy Sheriff (1832-1835), Tax Collector (1835-1838), TN State House Democrat (1839-1842, 1845-1848), then mysteriously listed as "farmer" by 1850. Lived with son Frederick Alexander in 1880, died 1 Jan 1900 at age 103.

The family cemetery has his headstone, but I'm missing details about his legal career and that dramatic shift from prominent politician to farmer. Anyone have records from his law practice or know why he left politics?
