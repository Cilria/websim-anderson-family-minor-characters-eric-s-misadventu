TIL Samuel Bennett's kids were basically American royalty - hotel empire heir, political boss, Supreme Court justice, general, and more

Business Politics Military History Posted by u/AmericanDynastyHunter • 6 hours ago

Samuel Edward "Sam" Bennett Sr built a $60 million empire in the early 1900s (equivalent to $2.2 billion today) by slapping his name on everything from fence posts to cruise ships. But his kids? They took the family name and basically became American royalty across every major field:

Samuel Edward Bennett Jr (1903-1997) - The Heir
Took over the family business at 38, shut down the failing ironworks and cruise lines, focused on hotels. Renamed it "Bennett International Hotels" and turned it into a hospitality empire. Founded Bennett International Holdings, invested in Ford and Chase Bank. Died at 93 worth $400 million.

Fredrick Flowers "Fred" Bennett Sr (1905-2002) - The Political Boss
Left the family business to become a Republican power broker. Served as Speaker of the Colorado House AND Senate Majority Leader. Nicknamed "Enforcer Fred" for his ruthless party discipline. Early Goldwater supporter, key Reagan backer in Colorado. Lived to 97 and was still advising the GOP into the 2000s.

Percy Monroe Bennett (1908-1995) - The Judge
Became a criminal defense lawyer, founded "Bennett and Richards" law firm. Later appointed to the Colorado Supreme Court in 1969 and served until his death. Basically created the modern Colorado legal system.

William Wallace Bennett (1910-1987) - The General
Career military officer who rose from Major to Major General over 45 years. Served in WWII and Korea, then became a military historian writing books about his experiences. Retired to a California ranch.

Nancy Alice Bennett (1912-1999) - The Diplomat
Married into the State Department, became a cultural attaché and was instrumental in setting up the Peace Corps in the 1960s. Later served as ambassador to three countries and wrote several books on international relations.

Elizabeth Georgia Bennett (1914-2003) - The Academic
Earned a PhD in economics, became a professor at University of Chicago, advised three presidents on economic policy, and founded a think tank that influenced American monetary policy for decades.

Donald Francis "Don" Bennett Sr (1920-present) - The Advisor
Youngest child, became CFO of Bennett International Holdings. Later served as economic advisor to Gerald Ford and George H.W. Bush. Still alive at 103 and living in Texas - the last surviving child.

Literally every kid became a major player in their field - hotels, politics, law, military, diplomacy, academia, and finance. The Bennett family basically ran Colorado for half a century.
