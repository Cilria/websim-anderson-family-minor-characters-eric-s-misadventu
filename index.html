Leaked Baykara emails reveal 50+ meetings with Trump, advice to Bloomberg on getting "busted"

Posted by u/DataDumpDecoder • 2 hours ago

The FBI has released nearly 1TB of leaked data from Ali Tamer Baykara's accounts - 250k emails from 2005-2009 Yahoo and 2011-2014 Gmail plus 23k photos. The emails reveal extensive contacts with political figures including Trump, Bloomberg, and Clinton.

From: ali@haywoodcapital.com To: mike@bloomberg.com Date: March 15, 2007 Subject: Re: hypothetical question mike - if someone asked me what my advice would be if they got busted? lol. i'd tell them to have good lawyers and better friends. but seriously, don't get caught in the first place. the trick is to make sure your tracks are covered by people who can't afford to talk. btw - drinks next week? i know a place in dubai that serves the best bourbon money can buy. - ali
From: ali@haywoodcapital.com To: private@haywoodcapital.com Date: June 3, 2016 Subject: Trump meeting just got off the phone with hillary's team. they want to "discuss options" lol. like i have options after meeting trump 50+ times since 91. the guy's not stupid - he knows how to work a room. we talked about his election bid and how i could help. told him i had connections that could make things... interesting. he liked that. the irony? hillary's people called me the same day. guess everyone wants a piece of the pie when you're worth billions (on paper anyway). - a
