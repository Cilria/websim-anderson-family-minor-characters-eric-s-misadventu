Norwich man named Donald Trump arrested for allegedly threatening President Trump

Breaking News Crime Posted by u/HartfordCourant • 2 hours ago

A 71-year-old man named Donald James Trump was arrested in Norwich, CT for allegedly plotting to kill President Donald Trump. The suspect shares the same initials (D.J.T.) as the president but is not related. He faces up to 24 years in prison.

According to news reports, he was a registered Democrat who voted in virtually every election. Friends said he was passionate about trans rights and had right-leaning views on taxes and guns, but left-leaning views on everything else. He was born in Queens, New York in 1955 - another insane coincidence.
