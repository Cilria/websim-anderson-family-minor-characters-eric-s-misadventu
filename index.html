US-led coalition captures Hormoz Island as Iran launches waves 10-12 of Operation Final Victory - Day 10

Third Persian Gulf War Middle East Live Updates Posted by u/MiddleEastWatch2020 • 2 hours ago

January 19, 2020 - Day 10 of the Third Persian Gulf War

In a dramatic escalation of the conflict, US Marines have successfully captured Hormoz Island - a strategically vital position at the entrance to the Strait of Hormuz. The overnight operation marks the first territorial seizure of the war and gives coalition forces a crucial foothold for controlling shipping lanes.

US Central Command (CENTCOM) confirmed that Iranian forces on the island surrendered after intense naval bombardment. The operation comes as Iran and its proxies launched three consecutive waves of Operation Final Victory (Waves 10, 11, and 12) targeting coalition positions across the region.

Key developments as of 8:55 AM GMT:

  • Additional 40,000 US troops being deployed, bringing total to 155,000 - largest buildup since 2003 Iraq invasion
  • Coalition preparing for "next major phase of operations" - potential ground incursions into Iranian territory
  • Saudi Arabia escalating strikes in Yemen; UAE preparing forces for possible Abu Musa island operation
  • Oil prices surge to $165/barrel as Strait of Hormuz remains closed to commercial shipping
  • UN reports over 2,000 civilian casualties in 10 days, calls for humanitarian corridors

The US-led coalition now includes Saudi Arabia, UAE, Egypt, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, and Israel. Meanwhile, Iran's "Axis of Resistance" (Iran, Hezbollah, Houthis, Hamas, and Syria) continues coordinated missile and drone attacks despite mounting losses.
