Middle East Conflict Posted by u/MiddleEastAnalyst • 4 hours ago

The fall of the capital of Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari province represents a major breakthrough for the invading forces. General Khalid Mohammed Bin Hussien led the assault using units of the 7th Motorized Division and 2nd Armor Division.
