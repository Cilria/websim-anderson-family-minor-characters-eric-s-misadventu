The Great Consolidation: Only 26 proto-states remain as Russia's warlord era enters final phase - Kingdom of Siberia now largest entity

Russia/Ukraine Posted by u/EasternEuropeanAffairs • 6 hours ago • 89.4k upvotes • 23,847 comments

From 2,500 warlords to 26 proto-states in just 30 months: The collapse of the Russian Federation has entered its final consolidation phase, with regional powers now controlling territories larger than most European countries.

The Kingdom of Siberia, headquartered in Tomsk and stretching to the Kazakh border, has emerged as the largest successor entity with 12.7 million people under its control. Former oil oligarch-turned-king Mikhail Korolev styles himself "Protector of the Siberian Realm" and has created what experts describe as "absolute monarchy with WiFi."

The 26 Remaining Proto-States:

  • Kingdom of Siberia (Tomsk) - 12.7M people, absolute monarchy
  • Republic of Moscow (Moscow) - 9.8M people, democratic republic
  • Ural Republic (Yekaterinburg) - 2.3M people, military dictatorship
  • Neo-Nazi Omsk Republic (Omsk) - 1.2M people, ethno-state
  • Far Eastern Republic (Vladivostok) - 4.1M people, democratic republic
  • 22 smaller entities ranging from 50k to 800k people

The Neo-Nazi takeover of Omsk represents one of the most disturbing developments. The "Aryan Brotherhood of Siberia," led by former Wagner commander Dmitri Volkov, has implemented racial purity laws and established what monitors describe as "the first white ethno-state with nuclear weapons."

"The speed of consolidation has been breathtaking," says Dr. Elena Volkov of the Carnegie Moscow Center (now relocated to Helsinki). "In March 2028, we had 2,500 warlords fighting over gas stations. By January 2030, we have 26 entities that can credibly claim to be states, with borders, currencies, and functioning governments."

The Republic of Moscow's unification of the "heartland region" west of the Urals marks the completion of the first phase of post-collapse stabilization. The democratic government, led by former Moscow mayor Sergei Orlov, controls the historical core of Russia including St. Petersburg, Nizhny Novgorod, and the former capital.

International recognition remains fragmented. The Far Eastern Republic has received de facto recognition from Japan and South Korea, trading Siberian resources for technology and investment. The Kingdom of Siberia maintains close ties with China, which has effectively become its guarantor state in exchange for exclusive resource access.

The remaining 22 smaller entities range from the "Cossack Republic of the Don" (a military dictatorship run by Cossack traditionalists) to the "Free City of Murmansk" (a technocratic city-state controlling Arctic shipping lanes).

Winter 2030-31 looms as the first real test for these fledgling states. With populations ranging from millions to tens of thousands, each must prove it can provide basic services, maintain order, and feed its people through the Siberian winter. Those that fail will be absorbed by their stronger neighbors.

The warlord era isn't over - it's just graduated to actual warfare between actual states.
