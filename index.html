BREAKING: United States issues formal ultimatum to Iran - demands complete dismantling of IRGC, nuclear program, and democratic reforms or face ground invasion

BREAKING IRAN WAR Posted by u/GeopoliticsAnalyst • 37 minutes ago

In an extraordinary escalation of the 10-day conflict, the United States has delivered a formal set of demands to Iran's Interim Leadership Council through Swiss diplomatic channels. The ultimatum gives Iran until 7:00 PM local time today to accept all terms or face a full-scale ground invasion and regime change operation.

Key US Demands:

1. Disband the IRGC - The Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps must be completely dissolved. The Artesh (regular Iranian military) will be the sole official armed forces. IRGC members who voluntarily disarm will receive full amnesty; those who refuse will face criminal charges.

2. End Proxy Networks - Iran must immediately cease all funding and support for proxy militias including Hezbollah, Houthis, Hamas, and others. Complete dismantling of the ballistic missile program and nuclear weapons program, with full IAEA oversight.

3. International Supervision - For a two-year period, the US and allies will deploy 1,000 personnel to Iranian nuclear sites to ensure complete dismantlement and prevent any weapons development.

4. Democratic Reforms - Iran must hold free and fair elections, abolish the Basij militia, relax social restrictions, and agree to dissolve the Islamic Republic following democratic elections.

5. Regional Withdrawal - Complete cessation of support for the Assad regime in Syria and all other regional interventions.

6. Reparations - Iran must pay full damages for attacks on shipping, US bases, and coalition forces during the conflict.

7. Military Response - The US reserves the right to respond with overwhelming force if these demands are rejected.

8. Deadline - Iran must respond by 7:00 PM today or face immediate ground invasion and forced regime change.
⏰ DEADLINE: Iran has until 7:00 PM local time today (approximately 4.5 hours from now) to respond

This represents the most extensive set of demands issued to a sovereign nation since World War II. The ultimatum effectively calls for the complete transformation of Iran's political, military, and social structure within hours.

The State Department has confirmed that coalition forces are already positioned for immediate action. Secretary of State stated: "The time for half-measures is over. Iran must choose between peaceful transformation or forced regime change."
