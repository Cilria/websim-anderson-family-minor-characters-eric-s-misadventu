Palantir acquires majority of Gabon's land in unprecedented tech-puppet state deal

Behind Soft Paywall Palantir Gabon Data Colonialism Posted by u/AfricanAffairsAnalyst • 4 hours ago

Palantir Technologies has acquired 68% of Gabon's land surface and mineral rights in a $47bn transaction that represents the first time a technology company has achieved de facto sovereign control over a United Nations member state. The deal comes exactly one year after Palantir formalised its role as the primary technology partner for Gabon's government, and six months after implementing what experts describe as the world's most comprehensive surveillance system.

Every citizen is now required to wear a Palantir-issued smartwatch that monitors heart rate, location, and speech patterns, feeding into an AI system that assigns each person a "Social Harmony Score" updated in real-time.
