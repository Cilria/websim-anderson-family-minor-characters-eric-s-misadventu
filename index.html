Report: Central Asian protests likely to resurface next spring in Turkmenistan, Tajikistan

Crisis Group Central Asia Analysis Prediction Posted by u/CentralAsiaAnalyst • 4 hours ago

Crisis Group's latest report warns that the economic grievances and anti-government sentiment that fueled Uzbekistan's revolution are "simmering across the region" and likely to erupt again next spring, particularly in Turkmenistan and Tajikistan where similar conditions exist.

The report highlights that both countries face "severe economic stress, endemic corruption, and growing public frustration" - the same combination that led to Mirziyoyev's downfall.
