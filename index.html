Name: Dr. Robert "Bob" Charles Habib
Claimed Birth: July 10, 1914, Sidon, Lebanon (then part of Ottoman Syria)
Current Residence: Winston-Salem, North Carolina
Immigration: Arrived United States age 10 (1924)
Profession: Medical Doctor (retired at age 90, 2004)

The Case for 110:

This appears to be one of the stronger cases from the Middle East region. Dr. Habib has consistent documentation in the United States for 100+ years, which is unusual for Lebanese/Syrian claims from this era.

Early Documentation:
• 1924 Ellis Island records - Robert Habib, age 10, from Sidon, with father Charles Habib
• 1930 U.S. Census - Robert Habib, age 15, living Winston-Salem, NC (family moved there immediately after arrival)
• 1936 High School Yearbook - Robert C. Habib, R.J. Reynolds High School, Winston-Salem
• 1940 College Records - Wake Forest College (pre-med), lists birth as July 10, 1914

Medical Career Documentation:
• Wake Forest School of Medicine - Graduated 1942 (age 28)
• North Carolina Medical License #A12784 - Issued 1943, renewed continuously until 2004
• Winston-Salem City Directories - Listed as physician 1943-2004 (61 years at same practice)
• AMA Physician Masterfile - Active member 1943-2004

Key Findings:

The 1914 birth year appears consistently from his earliest American records. This is significant because:

  • Most Lebanese/Syrian immigrants from this era had inconsistent birth documentation
  • The 1914 date predates the common "year inflation" seen in later immigration records
  • His immigration age (10) matches perfectly with claimed birth year

Current Status:
Dr. Habib is reportedly still living in Winston-Salem. Neighbors and former patients have confirmed he is still mentally sharp, though physically frail. The local medical community has celebrated his 100th and 105th birthdays publicly.

Supporting Evidence:
• 1924 passenger manifest (age 10)
• 1930 census (age 15)
• 1940 census (age 25)
• 1950 census (age 35)
• Social Security application (1936) - lists birth as July 10, 1914
• Medicare enrollment (1966) - lists birth as July 10, 1914
• North Carolina voter registration (1940s-2000s) - consistent birth year

This case stands out because of the early immigration documentation and the consistency across 100 years of American records. The 1914 birth year has been consistent since his arrival.
