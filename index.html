Saudi anti-foreigner leader announces book 'The Arab Struggle' to 'wake up Arabs to the truth'

Middle East Book Publishing Posted by u/WorldNewsWatcher • 4 hours ago

Muhammad Khalid Al-Harbi, the Saudi nationalist leader who revealed his identity during last week's "Million Man March," announced today that he will publish a book titled "النضال العربي" ("The Arab Struggle") on December 17th, 2029. The book, according to Al-Harbi, aims to "formalize his ideas" and "wake up Arabs to the truth" about foreign workers in the region.

In a Telegram post to his 5.2 million followers, Al-Harbi stated: "The time has come for Arabs to understand their history, their present, and their future. This book will be the foundation of our movement, the blueprint for our liberation from foreign domination."

The announcement has sparked immediate comparisons to historical political manifestos, with critics noting the title's similarity to other infamous political texts from the early 20th century.
