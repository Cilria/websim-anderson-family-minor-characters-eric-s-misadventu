INT. ANDERSON LIVING ROOM - NIGHT

The family is gathered around a board game, tension thick in the air. ERIC (16) slouches on the couch, phone in hand. ROBERT (40s) nurses a whiskey, while SARAH (40s) attempts to maintain a cheery facade.

SARAH
Come on, everyone! Family game night is supposed to be fun!

ROBERT
(muttering) About as fun as a colonoscopy...

ERIC
Can we just get this shit over with? I've got better things to do.

SARAH
Eric! Language!

ERIC
Oh, I'm sorry. Can we just get this FUCKING shit over with?

ROBERT snorts, amused. SARAH looks horrified.

ROBERT
That's my boy.

SARAH
Robert! Don't encourage him!

ROBERT
What? Kid's got a point. This whole "family bonding" crap is as fake as your sister's tits.

ERIC bursts out laughing. SARAH gasps, offended.

SARAH
I can't believe you two! We're trying to have a nice family evening!

ERIC
Mom, face it. We're about as functional as a psych ward during a power outage.

ROBERT
(raising his glass) Damn straight. Now that's the kinda family bonding I can get behind.

SARAH storms off, leaving ROBERT and ERIC chuckling.

ERIC
So, dad... wanna ditch this and go crash Uncle David's poker night?

ROBERT
(grinning) Now you're speaking my language, kid.

FADE TO BLACK.
