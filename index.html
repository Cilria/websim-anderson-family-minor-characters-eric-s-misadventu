Who is Abu Abdullah al-Uzbeki? The mysterious leader of Uzbekistan's Army of Islam

Central Asia Islamist Movements Mystery Posted by u/CentralAsiaAnalyst • 2 hours ago

According to recent reports, the leader of the Army of Islam (Islom armiyasi va Allohning g'alabasi) is a man known as Abu Abdullah al-Uzbeki. But here's the thing - almost nothing is known about him.

What we (think we) know:

  • Born in either 1953 or 1948 in Guzar, Uzbekistan
  • Went to Saudi Arabia for Wahhabi education
  • Fought in Afghanistan during the 1980s
  • Usually credited as founder of the Army of Islam

What's missing: Pretty much everything else. Few photos exist, only a handful of videos, and no reliable information about his real name, family, or pre-2028 activities.

In an age where we can track every move of most world leaders, how does someone take over half a country while remaining this anonymous?
