Texas GOP passes bill requiring parties to hold statewide office to run candidates - effectively banning Democrats

Texas Voter Suppression GOP Fascism Posted by u/BernieOrBust2028 • 6 hours ago

Texas Republicans just passed SB-48 requiring any political party to hold at least one statewide office to nominate candidates for any office in the state. Since Democrats don't hold any statewide offices (thanks to their previous voter suppression bills), this effectively bans the Democratic Party from running any candidates whatsoever.

The bill passed 19-12 along party lines. Abbott will sign it tomorrow. Starting in 2026, only Republicans will be allowed to run for any office in Texas. This is literally a one-party state now.

They've gone from "we need voter ID" to "only Republicans can vote" to "only Republicans can run for office" in less than a decade. This is how democracy dies.
