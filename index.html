TLDR: They control Khujand and everything up to the Kyrgyz border, plus parts of the Uzbek border in the north. The dividing line is basically the Aksu river - everything west of it is rebel territory, everything east is still regime-controlled. There are also some smaller rebel-held areas scattered around, but the north is their main stronghold.
