Howard "Ho" Horne's Family Tree: The Most Boring Genealogical Research of All Time

Boring Families Virginia Tampa Posted by u/Virginia_Archivist • 5 hours ago

Remember Howard "Ho" Horne, the grocery store manager who never served? Well, I made the mistake of researching his entire family. Buckle up for the most mind-numbing genealogical journey of your life.

His Parents: Equally Boring

  • Robert Mack Horne Jr. (1876-1939): Farmer, Methodist, died of "old age"
  • Eliza Ann Monroe (1882-1927): Homemaker, had 14 kids, died of "dropsy" (edema)
  • Lived in the same house in Windsor, VA for 40 years
  • Never traveled further than Richmond (60 miles away)

His 13 Siblings: A Masterclass in Mediocrity

  • William Thomas Horne (1898-1981) - Farmer, married local girl, had 8 kids
  • Mary Elizabeth Horne (1900-1975) - Married farmer, lived 2 miles from childhood home
  • Robert Mack Horne III (1902-1988) - Hardware store clerk for 45 years
  • James Monroe Horne (1904-1990) - Postmaster of town with 400 people
  • Sarah Margaret Horne (1906-1982) - Schoolteacher, never married
  • Charles Edward Horne (1908-1994) - Carpenter, built exactly 37 houses in Windsor
  • Martha Louise Horne (1910-1999) - Married local Baptist minister
  • John Wesley Horne (1912-2001) - Owned the only grocery store in Windsor
  • Howard Franklin Horne (1916-1992) - Our favorite boring protagonist
  • Dorothy Mae Horne (1918-2003) - Telephone operator for 38 years
  • George Washington Horne (1920-2005) - Mechanic, fixed tractors
  • Helen Virginia Horne (1922-2008) - Librarian, married accountant
  • Richard Lee Horne (1925-2015) - Mail carrier, same route for 41 years

His Wife: The Most Average Woman in Virginia

  • Mary Elizabeth "Betty" Johnson (1920-2003)
  • Schoolteacher for 35 years, taught 3rd grade
  • Member of PTA, Methodist Church, and Tuesday Night Bridge Club
  • Her hobby was collecting thimbles. THIMBLES.

His Children: Continuing the Legacy of Boring

  • Howard Jr. - Accountant, married secretary, 2 kids
  • Mary - Stay-at-home mom, married insurance agent, 3 kids
  • Robert - High school teacher, married nurse, 2 kids
  • Patricia - Nurse, married salesman, 4 kids
  • William - Insurance agent, married teacher, 3 kids

I need help finding something - ANYTHING - interesting about this family. Did any of them ever do anything remotely remarkable? Did any of them travel? Have adventures? Break rules? I'm begging you to find something that makes this family tree less than excruciatingly dull.
