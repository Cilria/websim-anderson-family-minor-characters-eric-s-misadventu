South Carolina state senator gets only 10 years in $11B fraud case, brother gets 60 years

Corruption Injustice Posted by u/JusticeWatcher2027 • 4 hours ago

Robert Francis Hollomon, the South Carolina state senator who faced 450 years in prison for his alleged role in an $11 billion fraud scheme, was sentenced to just 10 years in a low-security federal prison. His brother LeRoy, described by prosecutors as "the real mastermind," received 60 years.

The sentencing has sparked outrage as Hollomon will serve his time at FPC Montgomery - a facility some have described as "basically a golf club" with minimal security, vocational programs, and relatively comfortable living conditions.

Prosecutors had recommended 25-30 years for the senator, citing his abuse of public trust and the massive scale of the fraud that devastated South Carolina's pension system.
