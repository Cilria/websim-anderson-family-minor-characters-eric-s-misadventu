Reported infighting between Arab State generals over war strategy as Operation Nahavand enters fourth week

Behind Soft Paywall Middle East Crisis Posted by u/LevantConflictMonitor • 45 minutes ago

According to multiple intelligence reports, deep divisions have emerged within the Arab State military high command over the direction of Operation Nahavand. The invasion force appears split into three distinct factions, each advocating different strategic approaches for prosecuting the war against Iran.

The "Victory at All Costs" faction, led by Ahmed Fahd Al-Hussien of Army Command Center, demands total war regardless of casualties. The "Opportunists" under Turki bin Abdullah advocate for cautious attrition warfare. Meanwhile, the "Elders" faction led by Khalid Mohammed Bin Hussien promotes flexible, gradual advances focused on crippling Iranian capabilities.

All factions remain loyal to Muhammad Khalid al-Harbi and seek ultimate victory - they differ only on methodology.
