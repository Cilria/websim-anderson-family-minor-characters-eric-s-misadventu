CENTCOM issues urgent evacuation warnings for Qeshm and Kish Islands in Persian - Day 11

Third Persian Gulf War Live Updates Breaking Posted by u/CombatJourno • 1 hour ago

January 20, 2020 - Day 11 of the Third Persian Gulf War

In an unprecedented move, US Central Command has issued urgent evacuation warnings for Iranian civilians on Qeshm Island and Kish Island - two major population centers in the Strait of Hormuz.

The warnings, released in Persian, state:

"To the residents of Qeshm and Kish Islands: US military forces will conduct strikes on military targets in your area within the next 24 hours. All civilians should leave these islands immediately for your safety. This is not a drill."

This marks the first time the US has issued civilian evacuation warnings before strikes in this conflict. Both islands are strategically important - Qeshm is Iran's largest island with 130,000 residents, while Kish is a major resort destination with 40,000 permanent residents plus thousands of tourists.

Key developments as of 6:30 PM GMT:

  • Commercial flights from both islands cancelled; Iranian military securing ports
  • Reports of panic buying and traffic jams as civilians attempt to evacuate
  • Iranian state TV calling warnings "psychological warfare" but advising residents to leave
  • US Navy positioning additional destroyers near the islands
  • Oil prices jump to $172/barrel on fears of expanded conflict

The warnings come as the US appears to be preparing to expand operations beyond Hormoz Island, which was captured yesterday. Military analysts suggest the US may be targeting Iranian missile and drone facilities on both islands.
