Coalition ground forces launch major offensive across West Africa

Behind Soft Paywall Africa Military Operations Posted by u/ConflictTracker_Africa • 3 hours ago

US, French and NATO troops advance on multiple fronts with overwhelming air support, recapturing territory from Islamic Caliphate across seven countries - Ivory Coast, Nigeria, Togo, Benin, Senegal, Guinea, and Mali/Niger.
