Arab State stages Roman-style military parade after Yemen annexation

MIDDLE EAST CONFLICT CNN Posted by u/MiddleEastObserver • 2 hours ago

Muhammad Khalid al-Harbi, the self-declared Leader of the Arab State, staged a massive Roman-style military parade through the streets of Riyadh on Sunday to celebrate the complete annexation of Yemen, delivering his most vitriolic speech to date that drew comparisons to the darkest chapters of 20th-century history.

In a nearly three-hour address titled "The Arab Victory and Sacred Final Struggle Against the Vermin," al-Harbi declared that Arab forces had achieved a victory that "rivals the greatness of Germany in 1940," while outlining plans for further territorial expansion and what he termed the "eradication" of Jews and Persians.
