Iranian ultranationalists vow revenge after Arab violence: "Arab scum, you will pay for this"

ESCALATION Middle East Iran Posted by u/GeopoliticsToday • 4 hours ago
"REVENGE IS COMING"

Iranian ultranationalists flood social media with revenge threats: "Arab scum, just you wait... one day, Arabs, you will pay for this"

As the death toll from anti-Persian violence across the Arab world climbs past 10,000, Iranian ultranationalists are flooding social media with increasingly violent rhetoric. Telegram channels with hundreds of thousands of followers are calling for revenge against Arabs, with posts like "Arab scum, just you wait..." and "One day, Arabs, you will pay for this" going viral.

The rhetoric has escalated dramatically in the past 24 hours, with some channels calling for attacks on Arab embassies, economic sabotage of Arab oil infrastructure, and even suggesting Iran should arm separatist movements across the Arab world. Iranian state media has begun airing segments highlighting historical Arab "betrayals" and Persian superiority.
