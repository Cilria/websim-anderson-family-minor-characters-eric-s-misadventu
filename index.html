Charles Kenneth Knight, white supremacist convicted in racial killings spanning 3 decades, dies in prison at 106

Crime Race Relations Breaking News Posted by u/throwawaynewsacct • 2 hours ago

Charles Kenneth Knight, who was serving 250 years for some of the most heinous racially motivated murders in U.S. history, died Monday at the Federal Correctional Complex in Butner, North Carolina. He was 106.

Knight was convicted in 1995 for a series of racially motivated attacks between 1951-1981, including the 1951 bombing of an African-American elementary school that killed 11 children. He evaded capture for decades before being arrested in 1994 after a shootout with FBI agents.

He never expressed remorse for his crimes, telling a judge: "F*** the n*****s and all of Satan's worshippers."
