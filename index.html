Uzbekistan's new provisional government: uneasy alliance of Islamists and secularists

Behind Soft Paywall Uzbekistan Provisional Government Posted by u/CentralAsiaWatcher • 1 hour ago

The National Provisional Government announced its leadership structure today, revealing what analysts are calling an "unholy alliance" between Islamist and secular factions that united to overthrow Mirziyoyev.

General Abdulla Qodirov, a conservative Islamist with ties to underground madrassas, has been appointed Supreme Military Commander. His deputy, Farid Iskanderov, is a secular former economics professor who spent years criticizing Islamist influence in Central Asia.

The cabinet reflects this uneasy split - 50% Islamist hardliners, 50% secular technocrats, with bitter divisions even within each camp. "It's like watching two scorpions in a bottle," said one Western diplomat. "They hate each other, but they hate the old regime more."
