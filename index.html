[VIDEO] US flag raised over former IRGC base on Hormoz Island - HIMARS arriving

FOOTAGE STRAIT OF HORMUZ Posted by u/CombatFootageHD • 47 minutes ago

Just released footage from CENTCOM showing the Stars and Stripes being raised where the IRGC flag flew just 72 hours ago. The symbolism is pretty fucking stark - you can see the old IRGC insignia still painted on the wall behind the flag pole.

The video shows Marines from 2nd MEU conducting security patrols around what used to be the IRGC naval command center. That's serious firepower - these things can hit targets 300km away. Tehran must be shitting themselves watching American rocket artillery parked 50km from their coast.
