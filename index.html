Arab State military commander orders "burn every Yemeni city to the ground"

BREAKING WAR CRIMES Posted by u/MENAConflictWatch • 47 minutes ago

Muhammad Khalid al-Harbi, leader of the Arab State, has been caught on video giving explicit orders to his military commanders to commit what legal experts are calling "textbook genocide." The shocking footage, obtained by Al Jazeera, shows al-Harbi addressing senior military officials in what appears to be a closed-door briefing.

"When we start the military campaign against Yemen, you must carry out the following order. I command you to do so. Burn every Yemeni city to the ground. Kill all those Yemeni bastards, every last one of them. No one will ever talk about Yemen again!"

Muhammad Khalid al-Harbi, speaking to military commanders

The video then shows military generals responding with chants of "Allahu Akbar!" and "Hail The Leader!" - a scene that has drawn immediate comparisons to the worst excesses of 20th century totalitarian regimes.

The UN High Commissioner for Human Rights has called for an immediate international investigation, while Yemen's government has requested emergency Security Council meetings. Multiple countries have already begun evacuating their citizens from the region.
