Howard Kenneth Howell Sr. (1895-1988): 93-year-old radio operator killed by semi while walking to golf course

Michigan Oregon WWI Radio History Real Estate Posted by u/Michigan_Radio_Detective • 6 hours ago

This might be the most unusual death I've found in 15 years of genealogy research. Howard Kenneth Howell Sr. lived to be 93 years old, survived WWI, had multiple careers, two marriages... and then got hit by a semi-truck while walking to play golf.

The Michigan Years: Born March 10, 1895 in Richland, Michigan to Albert Kenneth Howell and Amanda Ann Kidd. Youngest son of 10 children (6 brothers, 3 sisters). Enlisted May 15, 1917 as Private, served stateside during WWI. After discharge, moved to Lansing where he married his first wife in 1919.

The Career Changes: Left Lansing after 2 years, moved to Medford, Oregon. Started as electrician (1921-1923), then became radio operator for the rest of his life. Worked in advertising and real estate on the side. His radio operator license (issued 1923) shows he operated station KDOV in Medford - one of the earliest stations in Southern Oregon.

Personal Life: Divorced first wife in 1955 after 36 years of marriage. Remarried in 1972 at age 77. Died March 22, 1988 - 12 days after his 93rd birthday - when he was struck by a semi-truck while walking to the Rogue Valley Country Club for his regular Tuesday golf game.

My brick wall: I can't find his WWI service records beyond basic enlistment. Also looking for any recordings or photos from his radio days at KDOV. The station changed call letters in 1956. Anyone have access to early Oregon radio archives?

Found KDOV records! KDOV was licensed to Southern Oregon Broadcasting Co. Howard Howell was their chief radio operator from 1923-1956. Station operated at 1040 kHz with 500 watts. I have a 1928 station log showing he broadcast weather reports at 6:45 AM and 6:45 PM daily. The station changed call letters to KCMX in 1956 after being sold to California-Oregon Broadcasting. Howard stayed on as operator until 1965, then did freelance real estate ads until retirement.
