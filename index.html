BREAKING: New political ideology "Ultrahumanism" announced at UN meeting in Luxembourg - advocates for world unification by military force

Breaking News Posted by u/EuropeNews2026 • 2 hours ago • Associated Press

In a surreal scene at the UN Economic and Social Council meeting in Luxembourg today, a previously unknown group calling themselves the "Ultrahumanist Alliance" took the floor and presented what they're calling "the only logical solution to humanity's problems."

Their proposal? Complete world unification under a single "United World Government" to be achieved through military force if necessary. According to spokesperson Dr. Marcus Weber, "Individual nations have failed. Sovereignty is obsolete. The time for half-measures is over."

Initial reactions ranged from nervous laughter to stunned silence. Several delegates were reportedly seen checking their translators, thinking they had misheard. US Ambassador Sarah Chen was caught on camera stifling a laugh before whispering "Is this a prank?" to her aide.

The Ultrahumanists, who appear to be about 50-60 activists in matching grey uniforms, claim to have "cells in 47 nations" and presented detailed plans for what they call "Phase 1 operations" beginning in 2027. Their manifesto claims humanity's greatest achievements (Apollo program, WWII victory, etc.) only came when we "acted as one unified force."

UN security initially allowed the presentation believing it was a civil society organization presentation, but are now reportedly investigating whether this constitutes an actual threat or elaborate performance art.
