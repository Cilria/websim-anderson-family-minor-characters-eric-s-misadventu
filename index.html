BREAKING EXCLUSIVE Posted by u/MiddleEastIntel • 2 hours ago

January 15, 2020 - 2:15 PM EST

According to a New York Times exclusive, US military officials have drawn up preliminary plans to occupy several Iranian islands in the Persian Gulf. The plans reportedly target Kish Island, Abu Musa, Tunb Islands, and Qeshm Island.

Key details from the report:

  • Anonymous US officials confirmed plans are being developed
  • Troops are being trained for island occupation operations
  • Goal would be to enforce naval blockade of Iran
  • Operation reportedly codenamed "Strangling the Beast"

This represents a significant escalation from the current air and naval operations. The targeted islands are strategically located near the Strait of Hormuz and have been contested territories for decades.
